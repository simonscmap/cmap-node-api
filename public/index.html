Data Acquisition

Simons Collaborative Marine Atlas Project (Simons CMAP) is an open-source data portal interconnecting data sets across Oceanography disciplines. It enables scientists and the public to dive into the vast and often underutilized ocean datasets to retrieve custom subsets of data.

Community
